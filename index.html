About Me

With a strong background in UI/UX design, level design, gameplay mechanics, and monetization strategies, I bring a holistic approach to game development that balances player enjoyment with business objectives.

Skills & Expertise

UI/UX Design: Crafting visually appealing and user-friendly interfaces that enhance player experience, designing compelling offer packs that drive player engagement and revenue, and developing seamless UI flows that ensure intuitive navigation and interaction.

Level Design: Creating challenging and rewarding puzzles for puzzle games that captivate players and keep them coming back, and designing dynamic and immersive levels for shooter games that offer a thrilling and balanced gameplay experience.

Gameplay Mechanics: Developing innovative and engaging gameplay mechanics that enhance player experience, and balancing game difficulty to ensure smooth progression for players.

Monetization Strategies: Strategically placing ads to maximize revenue without disrupting gameplay, designing attractive bundles and special offers that appeal to players and boost in-game purchases, and implementing various monetization methods to optimize revenue streams while maintaining player satisfaction.

Game Design Documentation: Crafting comprehensive and detailed game design documents that serve as the blueprint for development, ensuring all team members are aligned and working towards the same vision.
